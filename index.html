About

Original music and synthetic voices, shaped with care.

Techpule creates original electronic music, instrumental arrangements, UTAU voicebanks, DiffSinger models, and experimental vocal synthesis projects. The work blends emotional songwriting with technical sound design, treating voice as both instrument and interface.

From voicebank recording and tuning to polished releases, each project is built for creators who love expressive synthetic vocals, detailed production, and clean futuristic sound.
